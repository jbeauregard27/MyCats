Introducing:My Cats!!!

Midnight

Midnight was one of the first two kitties we brought home. Him and Sunshine were a package deal, living in the back yard of our new house. A couple months later and some fantastic bonding time, we were four cozy sitting inside.

Sunshine

Sunshine came along with Midnight. Her and her brother were abandoned together. We're not sure where, but they were microchipped and formerly named Wendy and McDonald. They each share an autoimmune disease called Eosinphilic Granuloma Complex Midnight has been in a cone since November 2023 due to lesions on his legs caused by this condition.

Evangeline

Evangeline was spotted by her mother as up for adoption by a local rescue. Her bright green eyes and adorable characteristics charmed right through the screen. After meeting her future parents a few times, Evangeline was adopted and has lived happily (unless sunshine is around)

Nizmo

Nizmo was found screaming her head off outside a Nissan dealership her father worked at. Aptly named after Nissan's racing brand, the little ball of energy has been the life of the house ever since she was brought home. While first being quarantined, she was known for non stop screaming if she knew a person was around. Shortly after being introduced to Evie, they've been near inseperable.
