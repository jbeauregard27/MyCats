Hello!

I'm Evangeline, but everyone calls me Evie. I'm a two-year-old gray tabby with a sweet and gentle nature. Life can be a bit challenging for me because my sister Sunshine likes to pick on me, and I get really scared when she's around. But I have my best friend Momo to keep me company. Momo and I are inseparable; we love cuddling and playing together. Midnight is also pretty cool. He doesn’t bother me, so we get along just fine. One of my favorite spots in the house is the hammock by the sliding door. It’s my little haven where I can feel safe from Sunshine and enjoy the great view of the outdoors. The birds and squirrels put on quite a show, and I feel at peace watching them. But as much as I love the hammock, my true favorite place is the catio that mama and daddy built for us. It’s the best of both worlds—I get to be outside without the dangers I faced in my past. In the catio, I have a cat tree that’s perfect for curling up in one of the cozy cubbies. It’s my little sanctuary where I can relax and feel secure. I can also choose to sleep in or on top of my donut, and there’s a double-layered hammock out there that Momo and I share. We have the best time lounging together, enjoying the fresh air and the freedom of being outside safely. Playing in the catio is like a dream come true. I can feel the breeze, smell the fresh air, and listen to the sounds of nature without any fear. It's my happy place, and I spend as much time there as I can. When I’m not in the catio or hammock, you’ll find me snuggling with Momo or playing with her. We have the best adventures together, and she always makes me feel safe and loved. Even though I have my fears, especially with Sunshine around, I know I have a loving home and wonderful companions in Momo and Midnight. My mama and daddy have given me a place where I can be myself, and for that, I'm truly grateful. Life is pretty good when you have a best friend, a safe hammock, and an amazing catio to explore.
